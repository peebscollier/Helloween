The Strange Green Light

You walk towards the ominous green light and find... a crashed alien space craft. There are two big aliens and one smaller one. To you they look like a family. The two older aliens desperately place their tentcle suckers all over the ship, trying to find what is wrong. The little alien is busy looking through bushes, a magnifying glass in one hand. Although you are scared, you decide to help. You call the AA and ask for the extraterrestrial department. They send out a lovely blue jellyfish guy called Dave-oid who fixes the ship, and the aliens thank you by suckering your face. They telepathically ask if you need a lift anywhere, and you get a light-speed free trip to your original destination, the Burley Inn. Time for a pint and a well earned sleep. You survived!
